Welcome to PUPCC Laboratories

The Computer Laboratories of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Cabiao Campus serve as essential learning environments for technology-based instruction, digital innovation, and academic research. Equipped with modern computing resources and software applications, these laboratories provide students with opportunities to develop technical competencies, enhance analytical thinking, and engage in practical learning experiences.

Through accessible technology, reliable computer facilities, and dedicated faculty guidance, the Computer Laboratories support the university’s commitment to producing competent, innovative, and socially responsible graduates who are prepared to meet the demands of the digital age.

To provide a conducive and technology-driven learning environment that supports quality instruction, practical skill development, research activities, and innovation in the field of computing and information technology.

  • Deliver clean, modern, and high-performance system stations.
  • Provide software tools matching international standards.
  • Maintain healthy, secure, and accessible learning environments.

To become a center of excellence in computing education and technological advancement by fostering digital competence, creativity, and lifelong learning among students and researchers.

  • Pioneer regional research in computing and networks.
  • Sustain a modern repository of academic hardware assets.
  • Cultivate self-reliant developers and network technicians.

The Computer Laboratories play a significant role in supporting academic and research activities by providing access to computing resources, software tools, internet connectivity, and technical facilities necessary for data analysis, system development, simulations, and other research-related tasks. These laboratories encourage innovation, collaboration, and the application of technology in addressing academic, institutional, and community challenges.

  • Facilitate capstone projects and research development.
  • Provide reliable hardware configurations for computational tests.
  • Encourage scientific investigation and knowledge creation.
